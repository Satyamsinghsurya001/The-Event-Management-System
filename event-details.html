REPUBLIC DAY 2025

Date: January 26, 2025

Time: 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM

Location: Red Fort , Delhi

Description: Join the biggest national festival,The Delhi Republic Day parade is the largest and most important of the parades marking the Republic Day celebrations in India. The parade takes place every year on 26 January at Kartavya Path, New Delhi. It is the main attraction of India's Republic Day celebrations, which last for three days. The first parade was held in 1950, and it has been held every year since. The cultural pageant is a symbol of a diverse but united India.
